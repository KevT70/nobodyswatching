THE DEAL

// 01

Sign In With Twitch

One click. No forms, no email, no personal info harvesting. We grab your username and avatar from Twitch and that's it. We're not interested in your data — just your stream.

*Seriously, we don't even have a database field for your email.
// 02

Build Your Profile

Add a short bio, pick your top 5 games, set your timezone, and link any other platforms you stream on. Takes about 60 seconds. Less if you can type fast.

*Bio writing is harder than it sounds. We know.
// 03

Get Discovered

When you go live, you appear in the carousel at the top of the page. When you're offline, you're still in the directory. Either way, people can find you — which is more than most platforms offer.

*We can't guarantee viewers. But we can guarantee visibility.
