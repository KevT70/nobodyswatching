PRIVACY

Last updated: March 2026. Written in plain English because legal jargon helps nobody.

// the short version

We collect as little data as possible. We don't store your email. We don't track you across the web. We don't sell anything to anyone. If you delete your profile, your data goes with it.

*That's genuinely it. But here's the longer version if you need it.

// what we store

When you sign in with Twitch and create a profile, we store:

Data Stored? Why
Twitch username Yes Your display name in the directory
Twitch avatar URL Yes Your profile picture
Bio Yes You wrote it, we display it
Timezone Yes Helps viewers find streamers in their timezone
Genres & top games Yes Feeds the directory filters
Platform links Yes So viewers can find your channels
Live status & viewer count Yes Updated automatically via Twitch API
Email address No We don't ask for it, don't need it
Real name No None of our business
Location / IP address No Not stored by us
Browsing activity No No tracking scripts, no analytics

// twitch oauth

When you sign in, we use Twitch's OAuth system. This means you log in directly with Twitch — we never see or store your Twitch password. Twitch sends us your public username and avatar, and that's all we use.

You can revoke our access at any time by going to your Twitch Connections settings and removing NobodysWatching.

// third-party services

The site is hosted on Netlify and our database is on Supabase. Both are reputable services with their own privacy policies. We use the Twitch API to check live status. We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other tracking tools.

Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts, which may log basic request data on their end. That's the extent of third-party involvement.

// cookies

We use a single authentication cookie to keep you logged in. That's it. No tracking cookies, no advertising cookies, no cookie consent banner taking up half your screen.

*You're welcome.

// deleting your data

You can delete your entire profile and account at any time from your profile settings. It's permanent, it's immediate, and it removes everything we have on you. No waiting period, no "we'll keep your data for 30 days just in case" nonsense.

// changes to this policy

If we ever change what we collect or how we use it, we'll update this page. We'll try to make noise about it too. But honestly, the whole point of this project is to collect as little as possible, so major changes are unlikely.

// contact

Got questions about your data? Want something removed? Find us on Bluesky or the Discord.
